About this course Skip Course Description
We think of Robotics as the science of building devices that physically interact with their environment.
The most useful robots do it precisely, powerfully, repeatedly, tirelessly, fast, or some combinations of these.
The most interesting robots maybe even do it intelligently. This course will cover the fundamentals of robotics,
focusing on both the mind and the body.
